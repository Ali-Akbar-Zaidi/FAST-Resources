IP Routing

This concise guide offers the basic concepts of IP routing, free of hype and jargon. It begins with the simplest routing protocol, RIP, and then proceeds in successive chapters to IGRP, EIGRP, RIP2, OSPF, and finally to the most complex, BGP. By the end, you will have mastered not only the fundamentals of all the major routing protocols, but also the underlying principles on which they are based. [Full Description]
